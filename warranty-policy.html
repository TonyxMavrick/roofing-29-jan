Warranty Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

At RoofEdge, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranty coverage. Every roofing project includes both manufacturer warranties on materials and our own workmanship guarantee. This Warranty Policy explains your coverage in detail.

1. Types of Warranties

1.1 Manufacturer Material Warranties

All roofing materials we install are covered by the manufacturer's warranty against defects. Coverage varies by product and manufacturer:

Asphalt Shingles (GAF, Owens Corning, CertainTeed)

  • Architectural Shingles: 25-50 year limited warranty
  • Designer Shingles: Lifetime limited warranty (50 years non-prorated, then prorated)
  • 3-Tab Shingles: 20-30 year limited warranty
  • Wind Coverage: Up to 130 mph wind resistance (varies by product)
  • Algae Resistance: 10-25 year StainGuard protection

Metal Roofing

  • Paint/Finish Warranty: 30-40 years against fading, chalking, and peeling
  • Substrate Warranty: 20-30 years against rust-through and perforation
  • Weathertightness: 2-year manufacturer installation warranty (when installed by certified contractors)

Commercial Roofing Systems (TPO, PVC, EPDM)

  • Single-Ply Membranes: 15-30 year material warranties
  • Fully-Adhered Systems: Enhanced warranties available (up to 30 years)
  • System Warranties: Include all system components (membrane, insulation, cover boards)

Tile Roofing

  • Clay Tile: 50 year to lifetime limited warranty
  • Concrete Tile: 50 year limited warranty

1.2 RoofEdge Workmanship Warranty

RoofEdge provides a comprehensive workmanship warranty covering installation defects:

Standard Workmanship Warranty

  • Duration: 10 years from installation completion
  • Coverage: All labor and installation defects
  • What's Covered: Leaks, improper flashing, installation errors, ventilation issues

Enhanced Workmanship Warranty (Premium Projects)

  • Duration: 25 years from installation completion
  • Eligibility: Available on complete roof replacements using manufacturer's premium systems
  • Includes: Annual inspection for first 5 years at no charge

2. What Is Covered

2.1 Material Defects (Manufacturer Warranty)

  • Manufacturing defects in shingles, tiles, or membrane materials
  • Premature granule loss on shingles
  • Thermal splitting or cracking of materials
  • Material deterioration beyond normal aging
  • Algae or fungus growth (if product includes algae-resistant warranty)

2.2 Installation Defects (RoofEdge Workmanship Warranty)

  • Leaks caused by improper installation
  • Flashing failures due to installation errors
  • Nail pops or blow-offs from improper fastening
  • Inadequate ventilation installation
  • Improper valley installation
  • Ridge cap or hip installation failures
  • Incorrect material application methods

2.3 Covered Repairs

Under warranty coverage, we will:

  • Repair or replace defective materials at no charge
  • Provide all labor for covered repairs at no charge
  • Match original materials as closely as possible
  • Ensure repairs meet current building code requirements

3. What Is NOT Covered

3.1 Exclusions Apply to Both Warranties

  • Acts of Nature: Hurricane, tornado, hail, lightning, earthquake, flood, or windstorm damage beyond rated wind resistance
  • Fire Damage: Damage from fire, explosions, or external combustion
  • Falling Objects: Damage from trees, branches, aircraft, or other falling objects
  • Building Movement: Damage from settlement, structural movement, or foundation issues
  • Improper Maintenance: Damage from lack of proper maintenance or neglect
  • Unauthorized Modifications: Damage from alterations, repairs, or installations by non-authorized contractors
  • Walking Damage: Damage from excessive foot traffic or equipment
  • Ponding Water: Damage from standing water (commercial low-slope roofs)
  • Chemical Exposure: Damage from chemicals, acids, or environmental pollutants
  • Condensation: Interior moisture or condensation issues not caused by installation defects

3.2 Additional Exclusions

  • Normal wear and tear over time
  • Color variations or fading beyond warranty specifications
  • Minor cosmetic imperfections that don't affect functionality
  • Moss, lichen, or algae growth (unless covered by specific algae warranty)
  • Ice dam formation (customer responsible for proper attic insulation and ventilation)
  • Damage to gutters, fascia, soffits, or other non-roofing components (unless specified in contract)
  • Interior damage to home contents, walls, ceilings, or personal property
  • Consequential or incidental damages

4. Warranty Conditions and Requirements

4.1 Registration Requirements

  • Manufacturer Warranties: Must be registered within 60 days of installation completion (RoofEdge handles this for you)
  • Workmanship Warranty: Automatically activated upon final payment and project completion
  • You will receive warranty documentation within 30 days of project completion

4.2 Maintenance Requirements

To keep warranties valid, you must:

  • Perform regular roof inspections (recommended annually)
  • Keep gutters and downspouts clear of debris
  • Trim overhanging tree branches
  • Remove excessive moss, leaves, or debris accumulation
  • Maintain proper attic ventilation and insulation
  • Promptly report any issues or concerns to RoofEdge
  • Keep records of all maintenance activities

4.3 Inspection Requirements

  • RoofEdge reserves the right to inspect your roof before authorizing warranty repairs
  • You must allow reasonable access to your property for inspections
  • Photo documentation may be required for claim evaluation

5. Warranty Claims Process

Step 1: Report the Issue

Contact RoofEdge immediately upon discovering a potential warranty issue:

  • Phone: (555) 013-ROOF
  • Email: warranty@roofedge.com
  • Include: Your name, address, installation date, and detailed description of the issue

Step 2: Documentation

  • Take clear photos of the problem area
  • Note when the issue first appeared
  • Document any water damage or related issues
  • Have your original contract and warranty documents ready

Step 3: Inspection

  • RoofEdge will schedule an inspection within 5-7 business days (emergencies within 24 hours)
  • Our technician will assess the issue and determine warranty coverage
  • You'll receive a written evaluation within 48 hours of inspection

Step 4: Authorization and Repair

  • If covered by warranty, we'll schedule repairs at no charge
  • If not covered, we'll provide a quote for repair services
  • Emergency temporary repairs may be performed immediately for active leaks

Step 5: Completion

  • Repairs will be completed within agreed timeframe
  • You'll receive documentation of all work performed
  • Warranty continues in full force after covered repairs

6. Prorated Coverage Explained

Some manufacturer warranties are "prorated" after an initial non-prorated period:

Non-Prorated Period (Full Coverage)

  • Manufacturer covers 100% of material replacement costs
  • Typically lasts 10-15 years depending on product

Prorated Period (Reduced Coverage)

  • Coverage percentage decreases each year
  • Based on percentage of warranty term expired
  • Example: On a 30-year warranty at year 20, coverage may be approximately 33% (10 years remaining √∑ 30 total years)
  • Labor costs are typically not included in prorated periods (customer's responsibility)

7. Transferability

7.1 When You Sell Your Home

Manufacturer Warranties

  • Most manufacturer warranties are transferable to new homeowners
  • Transfer notification must be submitted to manufacturer within 60 days of sale
  • Some warranties require a transfer fee ($50-$300)
  • Transferred warranties may have reduced coverage periods

RoofEdge Workmanship Warranty

  • Transferable to new homeowner at no charge
  • Requires written notification to RoofEdge with new owner's contact information
  • Remaining warranty period transfers in full
  • Transfer must be completed within 60 days of property sale

7.2 Transfer Process

  1. Notify RoofEdge of property sale: warranty@roofedge.com
  2. Provide new owner's name, contact information, and closing date
  3. We'll update our records and provide transfer confirmation
  4. New owner receives updated warranty documentation

8. Commercial Roofing System Warranties

8.1 Standard System Warranty

  • 15-20 year coverage on materials and installation
  • Includes membrane, insulation, fasteners, and flashing
  • Requires annual professional inspections

8.2 Extended System Warranty

  • 25-30 year full system coverage available
  • No-dollar-limit (NDL) coverage options
  • May include maintenance programs
  • Requires approved installation methods and materials

  • Emergency repairs to prevent further damage are covered if the cause is warranty-related
  • Temporary tarping or leak stoppage may be provided at no charge pending full repair
  • Emergency service fees apply for non-warranty emergencies

10.1 Disagreement on Coverage

If you disagree with a warranty coverage determination:

For unresolved disputes, both parties agree to attempt mediation before pursuing legal action. Mediation costs will be shared equally.
