Roof Replacement FAQs

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are completed in 3-5 days depending on size, complexity, and weather. We'll provide an exact timeline with your estimate.

Do I need to leave my home during installation?

No, you can remain home during the project. While it will be noisy, your interior won't be affected. We recommend keeping pets inside for safety.

What's included in the estimate?

Can you help with insurance claims?

Absolutely. We provide detailed documentation, work directly with adjusters, and help maximize your claim for storm or hail damage.

What if it rains during installation?

We monitor weather closely and protect your home with tarps. If rain is forecasted, we'll reschedule critical phases to ensure quality.
