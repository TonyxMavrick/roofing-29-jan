Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide an estimate?

Most estimates are provided within 24-48 hours of your initial contact. For emergency situations, we can often provide same-day assessments.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with leading financing companies to offer flexible payment plans with competitive rates. Ask about our financing options when you receive your estimate.

Is your estimate really free?

Absolutely. Our inspections and estimates are completely free with no obligation. We believe in earning your business through quality service, not pressure tactics.

How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most residential roof replacements take 2-5 days depending on size and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your estimate.

What's covered under your lifetime warranty?

Our lifetime warranty covers workmanship defects for as long as you own your home. Material warranties vary by manufacturer. See our warranty policy for full details.
